GlobeCarry

GlobeCarry is an innovative Android Kotlin project aimed at revolutionizing cross-border logistics through a peer-to-peer parcel delivery system. The application allows customers to input specific details about their parcels, including dimensions, weight, and destination, while travelers can offer their services by providing necessary proof of safety and reliability. This unique approach connects individuals who need to send parcels with travelers who can transport them, creating a collaborative platform that enhances the efficiency of parcel delivery.

To ensure a seamless user experience, GlobeCarry incorporates essential features such as parcel tracking functionality, allowing users to monitor the shipment's progress in real time. Additionally, the app includes a QR code generation feature, which facilitates efficient verification and ensures the authenticity of transactions. By streamlining the delivery process and providing a reliable, traceable system, GlobeCarry aims to enhance trust in peer-to-peer logistics while offering users a secure and convenient way to send parcels across borders.

TourAround

TourAround is an innovative mobile application that leverages augmented reality to enhance the tourist experience by helping users discover nearby attractions. With its user-friendly interface, the app allows tourists to identify various objects, such as statues of Buddha and Ganesh, through augmented reality. The application employs advanced object detection technology, utilizing TensorFlow and Python in Google Colab to train models that recognize these cultural landmarks. Once identified, users can receive the quickest routes to nearby transportation hubs, ensuring that they navigate their surroundings efficiently.

The primary aim of TourAround is to transform how tourists interact with their environment by providing intuitive features that facilitate exploration. By integrating augmented reality with real-time location tracking, the app not only enhances the discovery of significant landmarks but also enriches the overall travel experience. Tourists can immerse themselves in local culture while easily finding their way to attractions, making TourAround an essential companion for any traveler seeking to explore new destinations.

GalaxyGaze

GalaxyGaze is a Node.js project developed using React and Bootstrap for the front-end, featuring Firebase for authentication and database management. Users can securely log in with their Gmail accounts, allowing for a personalized experience. The application allows users to explore stunning Mars Rover images based on specific dates they select, providing detailed information about each image to enhance their understanding of Mars exploration.

The primary goal of GalaxyGaze is to offer an intuitive interface for users to engage with Mars Rover data. By collecting user details like name and email, the project aims to foster a deeper appreciation for space exploration and facilitate seamless access to captivating visuals from Mars.

TicketEase

This integrated web and mobile system, designed for TicketEase, leverages QR codes to simplify the recording of travel history. By scanning a QR code, users can easily manage tasks such as vehicle allocation, time management, ticket pricing, new vehicle registration, and updating employee details. The system is crafted to offer a seamless experience, ensuring that all administrative processes are handled efficiently via the administration website.

The web application component focuses on the needs of TicketEase employees, providing them with tools to effectively manage crowd control, route planning, and financial operations. Additionally, it facilitates the addition of drivers and users, making it easier to oversee all aspects of the service. This comprehensive system not only enhances operational efficiency but also contributes to a smoother and more organized travel experience for both staff and customers.
